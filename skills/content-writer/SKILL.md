# Content Writer Skill

Content creation skill for the Marketing agent. Generates blog posts, social media content, email copy, and marketing materials.

## Usage
Used by the Marketing agent for content generation tasks. Uses the LLM directly — no external service needed.

## Content Types
- `blog` — Blog posts with SEO optimization (800-1500 words)
- `social` — Social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram)
- `email` — Email marketing copy with subject lines
- `landing` — Landing page copy
- `pitch` — Pitch deck content

## Output
Returns structured content with variations where appropriate.
All content requires user approval before publishing.
